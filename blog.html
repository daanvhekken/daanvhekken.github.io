Programming experience
Absence requests system

At my previous education i was working on a application where you could keep track of the progress and presence of students. A part of this application was that students need to be able to request a day or more of absence. Me and my pair started working on this by creating a popup for students where they can enter a date, time and reason of absence. After this the teacher had an overview of all absence requests where they could chose to approve this absence request or deny it. Later on we added the option for students to see the status on their absence requests.
